The Problem

🔁 Repetitive Preferences

You tell every hotel the same needs—room type, temperature, dietary restrictions, special requests. Again and again. It's exhausting.

❌ Generic Stays

Hotels don't remember your preferences, even within the same brand. You get generic, one-size-fits-all service despite previous stays.

📊 No Data Ownership

Your preference data belongs to individual hotel brands. You can't transfer it. Each hotel starts from scratch with you.

The Solution: StaySync

A guest-controlled platform where travelers own their hospitality profile and selectively share it with hotels to unlock instant, personalized experiences.

✓ Your Profile

Create once. Room preferences, dietary needs, travel style, special occasions—all in one portable profile that goes with you everywhere.

✓ Selective Sharing

You decide which hotels see which preferences. Full control. You can share for one stay or multiple properties. Revoke access anytime.

✓ Instant Personalization

Hotels receive structured, actionable preferences. You get tailored service from check-in. No repetition. Better experiences. Higher satisfaction.

How It Works

1
